About S E E N

For K. Aymen Mebarki, the founder, the fragrance was conceived within a discipline of restraint and enduring refinement. S E E N is presence without excess governed by discretion, shaped by intent. Every element was composed with precision: the equilibrium of notes, the measured gravity of each accord, the coherence of the whole. The result is a scent that exists quietly, with authority.
